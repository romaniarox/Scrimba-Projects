Visit Mumbai

Capital city of Maharashtra and an alpha world city.
Also known as the 'City of Dreams'.

Top three activities to do at Mumbai

  • Awaken the scientist in you at the Nehru Science Centre

    • If you are a science enthusiast, then satiate your scientific curiosity at the Nehru Science Centre.

  • Get your dose of Vitamin Sea at the Juhu Beach.

    • Seek some calm from the hustle bustle by watching the sunset and taking a stroll along the beach which has a vibrant vibe & a carnivalesque atmosphere.

  • Feed your soul with creative inspiration at the Jehangir Art Gallery

    • Get inspired artistically at the Jehangir Art Gallery, which is a centre of activity for artists, connoisseurs of art, art lovers and public.
